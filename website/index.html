Our Story

Crafting Moments of Joy, One Cup at a Time

Steam & Bean began with a simple belief: that coffee should be an experience, not just a beverage. Founded by two friends with a passion for exceptional coffee, we've grown from a tiny corner shop to a beloved community gathering place.

Our journey took us to the misty mountains of Ethiopia, the volcanic slopes of Guatemala, and the lush highlands of Colombia. We work directly with farmers, paying premium prices for their finest beans, because great coffee starts with respect for those who grow it.
